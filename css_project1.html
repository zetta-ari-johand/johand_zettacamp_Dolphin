Invisible Boy

Debut Date: 29 February, 2023

Superpower: Super Strength and Invisibility

Invisible Boy possesses incredible super strength and the power of invisibility. With his ability to become invisible at will and his remarkable physical prowess, he stands as a guardian of justice. Invisible Boy fearlessly defends the city against villains and wrongdoers, using his unique skills to ensure peace and safety. Join Invisible Boy on his remarkable adventures as he fights for a better world and protects those in need! Despite his superpower, he's also invisible to the girl he likes.
