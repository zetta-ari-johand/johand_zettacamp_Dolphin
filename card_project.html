Face Cards

The big 4 cards

ACE

The ace of spades is the most powerful card in a deck of playing cards. It symbolizes power, luck, and triumph.

KING

The King of hearts in the Tarot symbolizes power, ambition, success and victory.

QUEEN

Queens are a big deal in the tarot. They are a symbol of leadership.

JACK

In card games, the Jack is often seen as a wild card that can be used to bolster any hand.
